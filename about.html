About The Team

We are students of Conestoga College in Ontario, Canada. We come from all over the world to meet for education and for a good cup of coffee. Our group comes from the Bahamas, India, Rwanda and Canada. We’ve sourced the finest daily-roasted coffee beans, the best local ingredients and serve them fresh to you. We were so impressed with our food we thought they were out of this world. So enjoy the best café Waterloo, this planet, or any other has to offer.
